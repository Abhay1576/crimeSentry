About the Crime Prediction System

In India, crime trends have been escalating in recent years, raising concerns for public safety. This system is built to study crime patterns, analyze demographic impacts, and use machine learning to predict future cases.

The site offers visual insights and predictive modeling based on data collected from the Indian government. It aims to identify high-risk crime categories and support the development of effective control measures.
