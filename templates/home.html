Predictive Analytics & Visualizations of Crime in India

Our Vision

ICA aims to facilitate the deployment of policing and law enforcement strategies by providing insights from crime data in India. Our platform studies crime trends and delivers predictions & visualizations for crime forecasting and strategic crime control.

Predictions

This module predicts future crime trends by analyzing patterns in various sectors including crimes against women, children, IPC crimes, and SLL crimes. View graphical representations by selecting crime type, year, and state.

Visualizations

Explore animated charts showcasing 15 years of crime data across Indian states. Factors like literacy and crime rate per lakh are incorporated to enhance understanding and analysis.
